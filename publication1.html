Abstract

This paper provides a comprehensive review of advanced neural network applications in educational contexts. We analyze recent developments in deep learning techniques and their potential to transform teaching and learning experiences. The review covers supervised and unsupervised learning approaches, reinforcement learning, and generative models with specific applications to educational technology. Our findings suggest that neural networks can significantly enhance personalized learning, assessment, and educational content creation, while also highlighting challenges related to data privacy, algorithmic bias, and implementation barriers in educational institutions.
